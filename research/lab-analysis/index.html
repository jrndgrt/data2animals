Lab analysis

LANUPRO analytical platforms for feed, additives, digesta, feces, animal-source foods, and biological samples.

LANUPRO platform work supports feed, additives, digesta, feces, animal-source foods, and biological samples. Samples are typically analysed in duplicate. When a project needs more than one readout, the laboratory can combine chemistry, structure, and biomarker assays into one workflow.

The platform grouping below mirrors how the laboratory actually clusters analyses.

feed digesta feces meat milk eggs plasma serum gut tissue
