R&D services

Lab analysis

LANUPRO analyses and customized experiments for feed, additives, gastrointestinal samples, animal-source foods, biological samples, and digestive physiology.

The lab analysis platform supports tailored analysis plans and customized experiments for feed, additives, animal-source foods, biological samples, and digestive physiology. Samples are typically analysed in duplicate, and additional processing or consulting can be added when interpretation or a broader experimental plan is needed.

Custom digestion and animal trial services

  • Product testing
  • Animal performance and meat quality
  • Gut health evaluation
  • Rumen bypass products
  • Enteric methane mitigation
  • Toxins and detoxifiers
  • Meat and alternative protein digestion
  • EFSA registration applications

Feed and additives

  • Moisture by gravimetry
  • Crude ash by gravimetry
  • Crude fiber, NDF, ADF, ADL, and ADIA by Ancom
  • Crude protein by Kjeldahl distillation
  • Crude fat by Soxhlet
  • Gross energy by bomb calorimetry
  • Fatty acids C10-C24 by GC
  • Short-, medium-, and branched-chain fatty acids C2-C12 by GC
  • Freeze-drying by lyophilization
  • Lactate and ammonia by spectrophotometry
  • Peroxide value by titration
  • ORAC by fluorometry
  • TBARS by spectrophotometry

Gastrointestinal samples

For rumen fluid, feces, and other in vitro or in vivo digesta, the lab can combine composition, digestibility, fermentation, and oxidative-status readouts.

  • Moisture, crude ash, crude fiber, crude protein, and crude fat
  • Gross energy
  • Fatty acids C10-C24
  • Short-, medium-, and branched-chain fatty acids C2-C12
  • NDF, ADF, ADL, and ADIA
  • Phenol, indole, and p-cresol by SPME-HS-GC-MS
  • Freeze-drying
  • Lactate and ammonia
  • Celite
  • Gas production including methane, CO2, H2, and H2S by MicroGC
  • Protein carbonyl compounds

Animal-source foods

For meat, milk, and eggs, analyses can address composition, physical meat quality, and oxidative stability.

  • Carbohydrates by spectrophotometry
  • Crude ash, crude protein, crude fat, gross energy, and moisture
  • Fatty acids C10-C24
  • Short-, medium-, and branched-chain fatty acids C2-C12
  • Collagen, salt, nitrite, lactate, ammonia, and heme iron
  • Meat colour, pH, drip loss, cooking loss, shear force, texture, non-protein nitrogen, and sarcomere length
  • Peroxide value, MDA, TBARS, ORAC, aldehydes, colour stability, dipeptides, vitamin E, GSH-Px, SOD, catalase, and protein carbonyl compounds

Other biological samples

For blood, gut tissue, liver, and related biological matrices, the platform includes composition and oxidative-status assays.

  • Crude ash, crude protein, crude fat, fatty acids, moisture, and freeze-drying
  • GSH-Px, SOD, catalase, MDA, FRAP, ORAC, vitamin E, and protein carbonyl compounds
  • Gut histology, including villus height, crypt depth, goblet cell density, and immunohistochemistry

Additional gut health readouts

The broader gut-health platform can also include microbial enumeration, microbial community profiling, histology, digestive enzyme activity, permeability markers, tight junction proteins, cytokines, chemokines, immunoglobulins, blood metabolites, fecal inflammatory biomarkers, and fermentation products such as short-chain and branched-chain fatty acids, lactate, and ammonia.
