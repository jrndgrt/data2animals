R&D services

Lab analysis

A working overview of feed, gut health, microbiology, histology, molecular, biochemical, and fermentation analyses available in the lab.

The lab analysis platform supports feed characterization, animal experiments, in vitro and ex vivo gut models, and gut-health evaluation.

Feed and ingredient analysis

  • Dry matter and pH
  • Gross energy
  • Proximate analysis
  • Macro- and micro-nutrients
  • Indigestible markers
  • Organic acids
  • Short-, medium- and long-chain fatty acids
  • Essential oils
  • NSP fractions and monomers
  • In vitro digestibility

Gut microbiology and fermentation

  • Ring-plate enumeration with selective media
  • Enumeration of E. coli, coliforms, lactobacilli, streptococci, and total anaerobes
  • Next-generation sequencing for microbial community profiling
  • Taxa relative abundance and density
  • Short-chain and branched-chain fatty acids
  • Lactate and ammonia

Histology, host response, and barrier function

  • Histological staining and microscopy
  • Gut histomorphology
  • Digestive enzyme activity
  • Gut permeability
  • Tight junction protein markers
  • Cell proliferation and apoptosis markers
  • Mucosal absorption and excretion
  • Transepithelial electrical resistance and short-circuit current

Molecular, immune, and biochemical readouts

  • RT-PCR for gene expression
  • ELISA for protein expression
  • Pro- and anti-inflammatory cytokines
  • Chemokines
  • Acute phase and heat shock proteins
  • Antioxidant and redox-system markers
  • Immunoglobulins
  • Growth and stress hormones
  • Blood hematology and metabolites
  • Fecal biomarkers for inflammation
