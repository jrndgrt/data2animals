Feed characterization

Analytical and nutritional characterization of roughage, compound feed, mixed feed, complete diets, and novel feed ingredients.

LANUPRO provides feed value analyses and interpretation for roughage, compound feed, and mixed feed. The work supports diet formulation and quality evaluation for cattle, pigs, and poultry.

Typical analyses include dry matter and pH, gross energy, proximate composition, macro- and micro-nutrients, indigestible markers, organic acids, short-, medium- and long-chain fatty acids, essential oils, NSP fractions and monomers, and in vitro digestibility.

The same platform is used to characterize complete diets and novel feed ingredients. Current and recent applications include enzyme strategies to improve nutrient use, evaluation of insect-derived feed materials, seaweed ingredients, and studies on the digestive properties and kinetics of feedstuffs in the gastrointestinal tract.
