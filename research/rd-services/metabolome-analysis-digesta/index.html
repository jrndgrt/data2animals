R&D service

Metabolome analysis of digesta

Integrated metabolite analysis for digesta samples, focused on volatile fatty acids, lactate, ammonia, and related fermentation readouts.

We provide metabolite analysis for digesta samples as a practical extension of microbiome sequencing workflows. The service focuses on the concentrations that matter most for gut fermentation and nutrient use: volatile fatty acids, lactate, ammonia, and related readouts.

Digesta chemistry

Quantify fermentation products that explain what is happening beyond the microbiome profile.

Integrated interpretation

Link metabolites to sequencing results, gut function, and production context in one readout.

Cost-efficient workflow

Plan sequencing and metabolite analysis together instead of treating them as separate one-off analyses.

PathoSense provides the microbiome sequencing side of that workflow. Their service covers long-read full-length 16S rRNA sequencing, shotgun metagenomics, tailored sample enrichment, bacterial and fungal identification, statistical reporting, and functional characterisation. In our collaboration, this makes it possible to combine sequence profiles with direct metabolite measurements from the same study in a way that is integrated and cost-efficient.

This collaboration is especially useful when microbiome shifts need to be interpreted together with fermentation outputs, for example in piglet digesta studies where short-chain fatty acids, lactate, and ammonia are important indicators.
