R&D service

Precision feeding and animal monitoring

Automated feeding, weighing, drinking, sensor, video, and data-analysis platforms for individual animal monitoring.

The precision feeding and monitoring platform is built to capture individual animal responses and convert raw sensor streams into decision-ready information.

Facilities include 10 pens with 10 weaned piglets each, fully automated electronic feeder stations for pellets, multi-phase feeding, electronic weighing stations, electronic drinking stations, controlled room temperature, climate sensors, and cameras.

Typical outputs include individual body weight, feed intake, water intake, feed efficiency, weaning stress response, digestive kinetics, nutrient digestibility, gut health, and diarrhea incidence.

The data workflow combines tailored statistics pipelines, R and Python tools, open-source methods, and AI, machine learning, and deep learning models for sensor and video data.
