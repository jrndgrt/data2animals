In vivo pig experiments

Pig and piglet trials for nutrition, feed additives, gut health, weaning stress, sow performance, and field validation.

Our pig facilities are designed for controlled nutrition and feed additive studies in weaned piglets, with links to commercial farm partners for sow, weaner, and grower-finisher validation.

The controlled unit includes 32 pens with 6 weaned piglets each, dry feeders suitable for meal, pellets, and crumble, feed or water additive administration, multi-phase feeding, auxiliary feed mixers of 25, 250, and 500 kg, controlled room temperature, climate sensors, and cameras.

Measured parameters can include growth, feed and water intake, feed efficiency, weaning stress response, digestive kinetics, nutrient digestibility, gut health, and diarrhea incidence.

Field-trial parameters can include sow prolificacy, lactation efficiency, farrowing duration, colostrum intake, piglet milk replacer intake, creep feed intake, carcass weight, and lean meat percentage.
