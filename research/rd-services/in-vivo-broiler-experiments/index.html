R&D service

In vivo broiler experiments

Controlled broiler trials for performance, diet quality, early-life nutrition, heat stress, gut health, and EFSA-oriented feed additive efficacy studies.

Our broiler facilities support contract research and academic experiments, from small controlled studies to larger validation work with commercial farm partners.

Available infrastructure includes one unit with 36 pens for 20 broilers each, a thermoneutral control unit with 8 pens for 20 broilers each, additive administration through feed or water, multi-phase feeding, Roxell Minimax feeder pans, metabolism cages for fecal digesta collection, controlled temperature and humidity, lab-scale setters for egg incubation trials, connected climate and eggshell sensors, weighing scales, and cameras.

Measured parameters can include growth, feed and water intake, feed efficiency, heat stress response, carcass composition, meat quality, digestive kinetics, nutrient digestibility, nutrient excretion and accretion, bone mineralization and breaking strength, footpad dermatitis, enteritis, gut health, and meat myopathies.

The same platform is used for early-life nutrition and hatching studies, including egg weight dynamics, eggshell temperature, breakout losses, hatching window, chick quality, chick activity, and early feed intake.
