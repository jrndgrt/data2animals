Research

Research themes, projects, R&D services, and lab analysis platforms from the Data2Animals group.

Themes

Broad research areas that organize our work.

Computer vision and sensing

Projects

Active and recent research projects.

CoolChicks

2026-06-11

Innovative rearing concepts for resilient piglets

A project on practical rearing concepts that improve piglet resilience around birth and weaning.

2026-06-11

Speenmanagement

NutriTwin

A digital-twin project for data-driven feeding strategies that support more sustainable and profitable pig production.

2026-06-09

Smarter protein nutrition through real-time monitoring

An Adisseo Research Grant project developing real-time monitoring for protein efficiency in weaned piglets.

R&D services

Facilities, protocols, parameters, and experimental platforms for in vitro, ex vivo, and in vivo studies.

Fermentation metabolite analysis

Integrated fermentation metabolite analysis for digesta samples, focused on volatile fatty acids, lactate, ammonia, and related readouts.

Pig and broiler experiments designed for feed additive efficacy evaluation, including studies aligned with EFSA guidance.

2024-12-29

Ex vivo gut models

In vitro and ex vivo gut models for digestion, permeability, mucosal response, antimicrobial activity, and microbial fermentation studies.

2024-12-29

Feed additive screening and development

Screening and development of antimicrobial, antioxidant, nutritional, and gut-health oriented feed additive concepts.

2024-12-29

Feed characterization

In vivo broiler experiments

Controlled broiler trials for performance, diet quality, early-life nutrition, heat stress, gut health, and EFSA-oriented feed additive efficacy studies.
