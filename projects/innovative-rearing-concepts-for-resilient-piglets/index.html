Innovative rearing concepts for resilient piglets

A project on practical rearing concepts that improve piglet resilience around birth and weaning.

Research project

This project addresses two recurring challenges in modern piglet production: high perinatal mortality in large litters and the abrupt transition at weaning. The work introduces and optimizes practical rearing concepts that help piglets become more resilient before and after weaning.

Piglet rearing and weaning

Improving the transition from birth to weaning

The project explores interventions such as alternate suckling, targeted drenching with bioactive substances, and pre-weaning group rearing in the farrowing room. The aim is to support vitality, robustness, and smoother post-weaning performance.
