Completed project

Practical concepts for more resilient piglets

This project addressed major challenges in Flemish piglet production: high perinatal mortality, the management of larger litters, and the performance drop and infection risk that can follow abrupt weaning.

The work focused on introducing and optimizing practical rearing concepts that can support piglets before and around weaning. The main strategies were interventions for neonatal piglets, such as alternating suckling and targeted drenching of bioactive substances, and pre-weaning preparation through group rearing in the farrowing unit with stimulation of foraging behaviour.

The official Varkensloket page brings together the project explanation, webinars and practical resources.
