CoolChicks

A VLAIO-supported project that developed a science-based heat action plan for Flemish poultry farms.

Started 01 December 2021

Heat action planning for poultry farms

CoolChicks developed a scientific basis for farm-specific heat action plans in the Flemish poultry sector. The project evaluated how feed, water, climate control, housing and management measures can reduce severe heat stress, both separately and in combination.

The work resulted in a prediction module and online tool that help indicate when a heat action plan should be activated. The tool can be adapted to the local situation on a farm and supports measures that are effective, economically relevant and practically feasible.

The Pluimveeloket page brings the main project outputs together, including a tool for estimating heat-stress risk and an action plan for mitigating heat stress.
