CoolChicks

A heat-stress project that helps the Flemish poultry sector turn weather risk into practical action on farm.

CoolChicks focuses on heat stress in poultry and its effects on bird welfare, performance, and farm economics. The project develops company-specific heat action plans and an online tool that helps farms choose practical measures before, during, and after heat stress events.

Poultry and climate stress

From risk to a heat action plan

The project brings together knowledge on ventilation, watering, feed strategy, and stall management so that poultry farms can respond in a structured way when temperatures rise.
