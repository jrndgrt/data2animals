'Speenmanagement' (ended 30 June 2022)

A completed practice-oriented project on improving piglet health and performance during the weaning transition.

01 July 2020 to 30 June 2022

Improving the weaning transition

Speenmanagement is the Dutch term for weaning management. The project focused on a difficult period in pig production: the transition from sow milk to independent feeding, new housing, new pen mates, and a more complex microbial and social environment.

The project translated practical knowledge into farm tools for improving piglet health and performance after weaning. The main attention points were management before and after weaning, biosecurity, feed and water, housing, monitoring, and structured improvement using the Plan, Do, Check, Act approach.
