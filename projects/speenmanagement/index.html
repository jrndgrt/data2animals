Speenmanagement

A practical project on weaning management that helps farms improve piglet health and performance.

Speenmanagement studies why weaning is not equally smooth on every farm and identifies the points where management can be improved. The project uses a practical PDCA approach to support better post-weaning health and performance without preventive antibiotics or therapeutic zinc oxide.

Weaning management

From risk factors to practical tools

The project works with a validated weaning tool developed by DGZ and Inagro. It looks at sow and piglet management, biosecurity, feed, water quality, and housing to improve the transition at weaning.
